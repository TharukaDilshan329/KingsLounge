Our Story

The Story of King's Lounge

King's Lounge was born from a simple dream — to create a place where families in Walasmulla and beyond could gather over exceptional food in a setting that feels both special and welcoming. Founded in 2014 by the Perera family, our restaurant began as a modest eatery serving traditional rice and curry to local patrons.

Over the years, word spread about our commitment to quality, our generous portions, and the genuine warmth with which we treat every guest. Today, King's Lounge has evolved into one of the region's most beloved dining destinations, while staying true to the values that started it all.

From intimate family dinners to grand celebrations and corporate gatherings, we've had the privilege of being part of countless memorable moments. Each dish we serve carries the legacy of authentic Sri Lankan cooking, prepared with love and the finest local ingredients.

Mission & Vision

Our Mission

To deliver an exceptional dining experience that celebrates Sri Lankan culinary heritage while providing warm, personalized hospitality that makes every guest feel like royalty. We strive to create a space where food, family, and friendship come together seamlessly.

Our Vision

To be recognized as the premier dining destination in southern Sri Lanka — a place synonymous with quality, authenticity, and unforgettable experiences. We envision King's Lounge as a landmark where locals and travelers alike discover the true taste of Sri Lankan hospitality.
